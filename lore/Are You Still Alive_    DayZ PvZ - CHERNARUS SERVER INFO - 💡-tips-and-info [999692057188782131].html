Dwight 11/2/2022 8:03 PM
ANOMALIES Anomalies are stationary but pose an incredible danger to those who are unaware. Anything that gets close to one will trigger a potentially lethal discharge of energy, this includes zombies, mutants and survivors. An anomaly may produce an artifact on the ground nearby when it discharges. These artifacts are highly sought after and are very valuable when sold to interested parties. The Artifact Trader is the broker for these transactions. As these artifacts are of unknown origin most people are uncomfortable being around then for to long, the Artifact Trader knows this and will often change locations before his welcome with the locals wears out. Artifacts will only be purchased if they are in a proper storage vessel. The artifact detector is an invaluable tool for finding artifacts. Once the detector is turned on it will beep and indicate direction when near a artifact producing anomaly. The detector will stop beeping once the anomaly has produced an artifact. Those who approach carefully can trigger the anomaly without damage, but get too close and the energy release can kill a survivor. (edited)
Dwight 11/5/2022 8:25 AM
STORMS Recently survivors have been reporting a frightening new phenomenon, one that they have dubbed the "Blood Storm". It starts as a normal storm but soon lighting begins to intensify. More and more lighting strikes build into an overwhelming crescendo of sound and light that causes even the bravest of survivors to cower in fear. Then the pulses begin. The first pulse slams into the survivors like a giant wave, knocking them back in a stagger. The next pulse and those that follow increase in intensity, often resulting in confusion and blurred vision. The final pulse, as the survivors say, is like getting hit by a truck. Nearly everyone loses consciousness. Those that were lucky enough to find themselves indoors during this final pulse often awake with nothing more than a headache and bloody nose. The healthy strong survivors unfortunate enough to be outdoors during the final pulse wake to physical pain and injuries. Those who are weak and outdoors.. don't wake up.
